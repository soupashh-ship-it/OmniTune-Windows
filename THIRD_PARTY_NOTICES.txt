Bundled native audio runtime
----------------------------
When packaged by scripts/release/build-windows-release.ps1 or the Compose
native distribution tasks on a machine with VLC available, OmniTune includes a
private VLC/libVLC runtime under native/vlc.

The VLC runtime is redistributed under the terms supplied with the VLC
distribution, including COPYING.txt, AUTHORS.txt, and NEWS.txt copied from the
bundled VLC runtime. VLC includes GPL/LGPL components and plugins; downstream
release owners must review the exact VLC build being redistributed and comply
with its source-code and notice obligations.
